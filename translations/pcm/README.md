# IoT for Beginners - A Curriculum

Azure Cloud Advocates for Microsoft happy to offer 12 weeks, 24 lessons curriculum wey dey all about IoT basics. Each lesson get pre- and post-lesson quizzes, written instructions to complete di lesson, solution, assignment and more. Our project-based way to teach dey allow you learn as you dey build, na beta way for new skills to 'stick'.

Di projects cover di journey of food from farm to table. Dis include farming, logistics, manufacturing, retail and consumer - all na popular industry areas for IoT devices.

## Pedagogy

We choose two pedagogy principles wen we dey build dis curriculum: make sure say e be project-based and say e get frequent quizzes. By di end of dis series, students go don build plant monitoring and watering system, vehicle tracker, smart factory setup to track and check food, and voice-controlled cooking timer, and dem go don learn basics of Internet of Things including how to write device code, connect to cloud, analyze telemetry and run AI on di edge.

By making sure say di content link well wit projects, di process dey more interesting for students and e go help dem remember concepts better.

Plus, low-stakes quiz before class go set di mind of student to learn di topic, while another quiz after class go help retention. Dis curriculum design make am flexible and fun and you fit take am for whole or part. Di projects start small and go dey more complex by di time di 12 week cycle finish.

Each project dey based on real-world hardware wey dey available to students and hobbyists. Each project look into di specific project domain, and e provide relevant background knowledge. To be successful developer, e good to understand di domain wey you dey solve problem, this background knowledge go help students think about their IoT solutions and learnings for the kind real-world problem wey dem fit ask as IoT developer. Students dey learn di 'why' of di solutions wey dem dey build, and dem go get appreciate di end user.

## Each lesson get:

- sketchnote
- optional supplemental video
- pre-lesson warmup quiz
- written lesson
- for project-based lessons, step-by-step guides on how to build the project
- knowledge checks
- challenge
- supplemental reading
- assignment
- [post-lesson quiz](https://ff-quizzes.netlify.app/en/)

> **A note about quizzes**: All quizzes dey inside the quiz-app folder, total 48 quizzes with three questions each. Dem link am inside the lessons but the quiz app fit run locally or you fit deploy am for Azure; just follow the instruction for the `quiz-app` folder. Dem dey localize am little by little.
